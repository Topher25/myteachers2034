COMM 2084: Media and Society

Dr. Jennifer Mackay

Media and Society is an intriguing class that teaches about the history of the media in our world and the ever reaching effects that it has had on our society. This includes subjects ranging from technological advances to cultural influences from various people throughout the last several centuries. Professor Mackay seems to truly enjoy teaching her communications classes over the years and is a big fan of her cats. This class has given me a lot of good information on how various current and past events and inventions have affected society today and gave me a greater grasp of how media affects and changes society.
